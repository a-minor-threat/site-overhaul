An Opportunity to Revolutionize

We are currently looking for skilled, versatile, and energetic professionals to help get our start-up off the ground. Join our team and help us revolutionize African development. We would prefer that all of our team members be based in the DMV area (with the exception of our Field Researchers); however that is not at all a requirement.

Technical Co-founder / Technical Consultants

We're looking for skilled and creative professionals to join our technical arm. The Technical Co-founder and Technical Consultants will assist in the creation and management of the Addis Ideas mobile application.
