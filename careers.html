An Opportunity to Revolutionize

We want skilled, versatile, and energetic professionals to help get our start-up off the ground. Join our team and help us revolutionize African development. We would prefer that all of our team members be based in the DMV area (with the exception of our Field Researchers); however, that is not at all a requirement.

Technical Co-founder / Technical Consultants

We're looking for skilled and creative professionals to join our technical arm. The Technical Co-founder and Technical Consultants will assist in the creation and management of the Addis Ideas mobile application.

Role Description

  • Lead the creation and management of the Addis Ideas mobile application Deliver across the entire app life cycle--concept, design, build, deploy, test, release to app stores and post-launch support
  • Work directly with fellow founders, developers, and product managers to conceptualize and build an elegant user interface and experience
  • Keep up to date on the latest industry trends in mobile technologies
  • Explain technologies and solutions to technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • Attend industry events/conferences -- both attending and presenting

Essential and Desired Skills

  • Fluent in HTML5
  • Strong OO design and programming skills in Java (J2EE/J2ME)
  • Knowledge of SQLite, MySQL or similar database management system
  • Familiar with Eclipse
  • Excellent debugging and optimization skills
  • Previous Startup experience would be considered a plus
  • Familiar with the Android SDK would be considered a plus

Competencies for Success

  • Versatility
  • High Energy
  • Time Management
  • Strong Communication Skills--both oral and written
  • Passionate about Africa and/or International Development

Compensation

  • 10-20% equity commensurate with experience and desired role
